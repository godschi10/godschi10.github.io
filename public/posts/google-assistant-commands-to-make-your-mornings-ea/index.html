    19 Google Assistant commands to make your mornings easier

    October 30, 2023 · 6 min

    Are you tired of starting your day feeling disorganized and rushed? Do you wish there was a way to streamline your morning routine, so you can start your day off right? You're not alone. Many of us struggle with getting our day started smoothly and efficiently.

    The good news is, there's a solution that's already at your fingertips. Google Assistant, a powerful virtual assistant, can help you organize your mornings with simple voice commands. From setting alarms and checking the weather to finding recipes and reading the news, Google Assistant can do it all.

    In this blog post, I'll share a variety of Google Assistant commands that will transform your mornings, dividing them into four sections based on the categories of the commands: Information, Productivity, Entertainment, and Smart Home.

    So, let's get started!

    Information

    In the age of information, having the right details at your fingertips can make all the difference, especially in the morning when you're planning your day.

    Google Assistant can serve as your personal information hub, providing you with all the essential details you need to start your day right.

    Remember to use these commands as a starting point and feel free to explore other commands that might suit your needs better.

    Here are some commands that can help you stay informed:

    "Good morning"

    This command will give you a summary of the day's weather, traffic, and calendar events.

    It's like having a personal assistant who knows exactly what you need to know as soon as you wake up.

    "What's the weather today?"

    This command will give you a detailed weather forecast for your location.

    Whether you're deciding what to wear or whether to carry an umbrella, this command has got you covered.

    "What's on my calendar today?"

    This command will tell you what events you have scheduled for the day.

    "What are the news headlines?"

    This command will give you a summary of the latest news headlines so you can stay updated with the latest happenings around the world while you enjoy your morning coffee.

    "What's the traffic like?"

    This command will give you a traffic report for your area so you can plan your commute and avoid traffic jams with this handy command.

    Ask questions and satisfy those early morning curiosities

    For instance, if you've ever wondered who invented coffee, just ask Google Assistant!

    So next time a random question pops into your head, remember that Google Assistant is there to help!

    Productivity

    In our fast-paced world, productivity is key, especially in the morning when we're setting the tone for the rest of the day. Google Assistant can help you stay on top of your tasks and manage your time more effectively. Here are some commands that can boost your productivity:

    "Set an alarm for [time]"

    This command will set an alarm on your phone. Whether you need a reminder for a meeting or you're timing your morning workout, Google Assistant has got you covered.

    "Add an event to my calendar for [time] on [date]"

    This command will add an event to your calendar. Never forget an important date or appointment again!

    "Send a text to [contact name] that says [message]"

    This command will send a text message to the specified contact. Stay connected with your friends and family without even touching your phone.

    "Make a call to [contact name]"

    This command will make a phone call to the specified contact. Whether it's a business call or catching up with a loved one, Google Assistant makes it easy.

    "Play a podcast on [podcast name]"

    This command will play the specified podcast episode. Make your morning commute or workout more enjoyable with your favorite podcasts.

    Entertainment

    In the morning, a little entertainment can go a long way in setting the right mood for the day.

    Google Assistant can play your favorite music, tell you a joke, or even read you the news.

    Here are some commands that can add a touch of fun and relaxation to your mornings:

    "Play my favorite playlist"

    This command will play your favorite playlist on your preferred music streaming service.

    Whether you like to start your day with some energetic pop music or soothing classical tunes, Google Assistant has got you covered.

    "Play a video on YouTube called [video title]"

    This command will play the specified video on YouTube so you can watch your favorite shows or learn something new.

    "Tell me a joke"

    This command will tell you a joke so you can start your day with a smile and a bit of laughter!

    "Read me the news"

    This command will read you the latest news headlines so you can stay informed about the world around you as you enjoy your morning coffee.

    Smart Home

    In the era of smart homes, our living spaces are becoming more connected and intelligent.

    A smart home is a setup where internet-connected devices are used to monitor and control various systems and appliances in your home. These can include lighting, heating, security systems, and even kitchen appliances.

    By using Google Assistant, you can control these devices with just your voice, making your mornings more convenient and efficient.

    These commands require compatible smart home devices and proper setup. Once everything is in place, you can start enjoying a more connected and convenient home with Google Assistant.

    Here are some commands that can help you manage your smart home:

    "Turn on the lights in the living room"

    This command will turn on the lights in the living room. No need to fumble for the light switch when you have Google Assistant.

    "Set the thermostat to [temperature]"

    This command will set the thermostat to the specified temperature, keeping your home comfortable without lifting a finger.

    Start the coffee maker"

    This command will start the coffee maker. Imagine waking up to the smell of freshly brewed coffee every morning!

    "Turn on the TV"

    This command will turn on the TV so you can catch up on your favorite shows or news as you get ready for the day.

    Conclusion

    In conclusion, Google Assistant commands can truly revolutionize your mornings. From providing information and boosting productivity to offering entertainment and managing your smart home, these commands can make your mornings smoother and more enjoyable.

    You can try out some of these commands and share your feedback or recommendations.
