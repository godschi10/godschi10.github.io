<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>It can be frustrating trying to figure out what's behind those deleted messages on your Whatsapp chats. </p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>I'm talking about those “This message was deleted” texts that appear after someone un-sends their message to you on WhatsApp.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:image {"id":6857,"sizeSlug":"full","linkDestination":"media"} -->

<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Once the message is deleted you can never see it again.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>But rest assured, I found an easy way that has been helping me read all deleted messages in my WhatsApp chats, and I will share it with you in this article.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Let's get right into it.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Go to Playstore and download Notisave</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Head over to Playstore, search "<a href="https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.tenqube.notisave">Notisave</a>", install the app, then open it.</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Open the Notisave app and run some quick settings</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Before you can start using this app, you need to make some important settings, which are allowing notification access, allowing access to media/file storage and turning off blocked notifications for all apps.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">2. Turn off blocked notifications for all apps</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Notisave blocks your notification by default. Turn off blocked notifications for all apps, so you can see all your notifications as you normally would.</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">View deleted WhatsApp messages</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Whenever you receive a Whatsapp message that later gets deleted, head over to the Notisave app, look for the WhatsApp message section, select the contact you want to view their message and then click on it.</p>
