<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">"What's on my calendar today?"</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>This command will tell you what events you have scheduled for the day.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">"What are the news headlines?"</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>This command will give you a summary of the latest news headlines so you can stay updated with the latest happenings around the world while you enjoy your morning coffee.</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Productivity</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>In our fast-paced world, productivity is key, especially in the morning when we're setting the tone for the rest of the day. Google Assistant can help you stay on top of your tasks and manage your time more effectively. Here are some commands that can boost your productivity:</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">"Set an alarm for [time]"</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>This command will set an alarm on your phone. Whether you need a reminder for a meeting or you're timing your morning workout, Google Assistant has got you covered.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">"Add an event to my calendar for [time] on [date]"</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>This command will add an event to your calendar. Never forget an important date or appointment again!</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">"Send a text to [contact name] that says [message]"</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>This command will send a text message to the specified contact. Stay connected with your friends and family without even touching your phone.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">"Make a call to [contact name]"</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>This command will make a phone call to the specified contact. Whether it's a business call or catching up with a loved one, Google Assistant makes it easy.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">"Play a podcast on [podcast name]"</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>This command will play the specified podcast episode. Make your morning commute or workout more enjoyable with your favorite podcasts.</p>
